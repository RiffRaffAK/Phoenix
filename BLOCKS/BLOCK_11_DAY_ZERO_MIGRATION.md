Day Zero Migration defines the complete strategy and procedures for onboarding existing systems, users, data, and financial records into the Phoenix Wolf Systems platform with zero downtime and full data integrity.

- Preserve all historical data with accurate timestamps
- Maintain continuity of financial ledgers and wage records
- Transfer users with no interruption to access or services
- Apply Constitutional Values retroactively from system genesis
- Establish full audit trails from the moment of migration
