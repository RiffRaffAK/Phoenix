# BLOCK 5: Cave Ownership & Business Management

## Overview
The Cave Ownership system establishes the rights, responsibilities, and governance framework for business entities ("caves") operating within the Phoenix Wolf Systems. A cave is the fundamental unit of business ownership in the system.

## Cave Owner Responsibilities
- Maintaining compliance with all 25 Constitutional Values
- Ensuring fair wages and timely payment to all workers
- Operating within the 22 Dysfunctional Rules framework
- Filing accurate revenue reports for royalty calculations
- Upholding child safety protocols at all times
- Participating in the self-auditing infinite system

## Business Management Strategies
- **Real-Time Ledger**: All transactions recorded with nanosecond precision
- **Dual Wallet Integration**: Business earnings tracked in Wallet A; UBI contributions tracked in Wallet B
- **Triple-Layer Voting**: Major business decisions subject to community vote
- **Automated Compliance**: Constitutional checks run on every significant action
- **Threat Detection**: 8-microsecond latency monitoring for security incidents

## Ownership Rights and Structures
- **Solo Cave**: Single owner, full control and full liability
- **Partnership Cave**: Two or more owners with documented share structure
- **Community Cave**: Collectively owned by den members with democratic governance
- **Franchise Cave**: Licensed from a parent cave with royalty obligations (30-80%)

## Cave Registration Requirements
1. Owner identity verified through Electrical Handshake Lock
2. Business purpose declared and approved against Constitutional Values
3. Industry classification assigned from the 157-industry taxonomy
4. Applicable wage laws from relevant jurisdiction(s) loaded
5. UBI contribution rate calculated and configured
6. Initial audit trail opened in the self-auditing system

## Revenue and Royalty Framework
- Gross revenue tracked in real-time
- Royalty rate (30-80%) applied per cave classification
- Net earnings deposited to Wallet A automatically
- UBI pool funded by 20% of security bounty proceeds per cave
