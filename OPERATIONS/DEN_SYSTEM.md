## Key Features

### Child Safety (ABSOLUTE)
- Operates independently of all other DEN functions
- Cannot be disabled by any DEN member including DEN Leader
- Continuous monitoring with adjustable age-appropriate protocols
- Emergency beacon available to children 10+

### Support Calculations
- Child support automated by jurisdiction formula
- Alimony calculation based on income gap and marriage duration
- Automatic payment scheduling from CAVE/UBI wallet
- Income change triggers automatic recalculation

### Inheritance Management
- Immutable will records (hash-stored)
- Death protocol with fraud prevention hold
- Inheritance theft detection (Rule 18, Red Signal)
- Genealogy tree preserved for all members

### Housing Stability Index (HSI)
- Continuous calculation for all DEN members
- Early warning connects support resources
- Emergency housing protocol for crisis situations
- Children's safety assessed independently
