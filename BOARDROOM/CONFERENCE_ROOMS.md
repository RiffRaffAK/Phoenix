# Phoenix Wolf Systems — Boardroom & Conference Room System

> **Status:** ACTIVE | **AI Facilitation:** Phoenix + Alaska | **Recording:** Consent-Based

---

## Overview

The Phoenix Wolf Systems Boardroom is the virtual collaboration and governance infrastructure for CAVEs, DENs, and community organizations. It provides structured spaces for meetings, decisions, and recorded governance actions — with Phoenix and Alaska available as AI facilitators.

---

## Conference Room Types

| Type | Access | Capacity | Recording |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Public Room** | Any member | Unlimited | Optional (consent all) |
| **Private Room** | Invited members only | Configurable | Optional (consent all) |
| **Executive Room** | CAVE Leadership / DEN Leaders | Configurable | Automatic + consent |
| **Emergency Room** | Crisis protocol | Unlimited | Automatic + consent |
| **Governance Room** | Voting participants | Configurable | Automatic |
| **Boardroom** | CAVE Board / Founders | Board members | Automatic + consent |

## Meeting Features

### AI Facilitation (Phoenix + Alaska)
- **Phoenix:** Constitutional compliance monitoring, decision documentation
- **Alaska:** Technical facilitation, time management, security monitoring
- Both available for facilitation on request
- Neither AI overrides human participants

### Recording Protocols
- Recording requires consent from ALL participants before it begins
- Consent request sent to all participants, must be granted individually
- Recording automatically stops if any participant withdraws consent
- Recordings stored encrypted, accessible only to participants
- Recordings deleted on request by any participant

### Minutes Automation
- AI-generated meeting minutes (Phoenix)
- Action items extracted automatically
- Decisions documented with vote records
- Published to participants within 1 hour

### Decision Documentation
- All decisions recorded with timestamp
- Voting results attached
- Constitutional compliance check on all decisions
- Immutable record for governance accountability

## Integration

- **CAVE Governance:** Board decisions feed into CAVE governance records
- **Voting System:** In-meeting votes integrated with Triple-Layer system
- **DEN System:** DEN governance meetings supported
- **Sovereign Voice:** Contract review sessions with legal analysis
- **Time Tracking:** Meeting time trackable as work time (with member consent)

---

## Guest Access

External guests (non-members) may be invited to specific rooms:
- Guest link with time-limited access token
- Guest cannot access other areas of the platform
- Guest identity verified to level appropriate for room security
- Guest recording consent required same as members
- Guest data deleted from platform within 30 days of meeting unless consent given
