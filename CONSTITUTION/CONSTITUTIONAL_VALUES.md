## Value 1 — Sovereignty

**Full Statement:** Every individual is a sovereign being whose autonomy over their own life, body, data, and decisions is inviolable within Phoenix Wolf Systems.

**Core Principle:** No system, no corporation, no government, and no other individual may override a person's sovereignty over their own life without that person's explicit, informed, and revocable consent.

**Enforcement Mechanism:**
- All consent is specific, documented, and revocable at any time
- No "accept all" consent waivers are permitted on the platform
- Consent revocation takes effect within 24 hours
- Any action taken without proper consent is automatically reversed

**Interaction with Other Values:**
- Sovereignty yields to Child Safety (Value 11) when a sovereignty claim would harm a child
- Sovereignty and Security (Value 7) are balanced: security measures require consent except in active threat scenarios
- Sovereignty and Accountability (Value 9) coexist: sovereignty over choices + accountability for consequences

## Value 2 — Justice

**Full Statement:** All decisions made within Phoenix Wolf Systems shall be fair, evidence-based, transparent, and free from bias.

**Core Principle:** Justice means that identical situations receive identical treatment. When outcomes differ, the reason must be articulable, logical, and free from discriminatory motivation.

**Enforcement Mechanism:**
- All automated decisions produce human-readable justifications
- All decisions are appealable
- Statistical audits run quarterly to detect disparate impacts
- Justice delays are tracked (justice delayed = justice flagged)

**Interaction with Other Values:**
- Justice and Compassion (Value 10) require each other: just outcomes delivered without compassion are incomplete; compassion without justice is favoritism
- Justice and Truth (Value 8) are inseparable: justice built on falsehood is not justice

## Value 3 — Equality

**Full Statement:** No individual shall be treated differently on the basis of race, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, religion, disability, age, national origin, economic status, immigration status, appearance, or any other characteristic that does not bear on the decision being made.

**Core Principle:** Equality requires equitable process. It does not prohibit recognition of historical disadvantage — corrective equity measures that lift marginalized communities are not violations of this value.

**Enforcement Mechanism:**
- All role assignments and CAVE hiring decisions are audited for bias
- Algorithmic Discrimination (Rule 10) directly enforces this value
- Pay equity analysis runs automatically for all CAVEs
- Quarterly equality audit published transparently

**Interaction with Other Values:**
- Equality and Inclusion (Value 18) are twin values: Equality ensures equal treatment; Inclusion ensures presence
- Equality and Economic Justice (Value 13) intersect: economic disparities along identity lines are equality violations

## Value 4 — Liberty

**Full Statement:** Every individual has the freedom to choose their path, their beliefs, their associations, their expression, and their way of life, within the boundary that their choices do not cause measurable harm to others.

**Core Principle:** The liberty boundary is defined by harm, not by discomfort. A choice that one person finds offensive but that causes no measurable harm to another is protected by Liberty.

**Enforcement Mechanism:**
- The harm boundary is calculated using a structured harm assessment
- Actions that only affect the actor receive full liberty protection (with support resources)
- Actions that harm third parties lose liberty protection proportional to the harm

**Interaction with Other Values:**
- Liberty and Child Safety (Value 11): An adult's liberty claim cannot justify harm to a child
- Liberty and Security (Value 7): Security measures may temporarily constrain liberty when threat is specific and credible, not as a blanket restriction

## Value 5 — Transparency

**Full Statement:** All system actions, automated decisions, revenue flows, and governance processes are auditable by relevant stakeholders.

**Core Principle:** Phoenix Wolf Systems does not operate in black boxes. Every consequential action produces an audit trail that can be reviewed. The system explains itself.

**Enforcement Mechanism:**
